CLIMATE ADAPTATION PARTNERSHIP FRAMEWORK

The scale we focused on for the framework was place-based, where national meets local; administrative areas such as cities, islands or regions within local government boundaries that have the power to implement adaption actions. For climate adaptation to be successfully implemented on the ground there needs to be buy-in from all stakeholders and place-based adaptation supports this.
